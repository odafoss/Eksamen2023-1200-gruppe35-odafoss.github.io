OsloMet

In the heart of Oslo you can find OsloMet or Oslo Metropolitan University. The univeristy is located on Bislet. With a modern campus and a diverse community of scholars and students, OsloMet is all about the progress and the learning. This institution is dedicated to creating graduates that work as teachers, social workers, nurses, engineers, developers and other professions, while empowering them to make a meaningful contribution to society.

As they state on their own website they are dedicated to sharing knowledge to move the Norwegian welfare state forward: "Through the students we educate and the knowledge we produce, Norway's most urban university helps drive the welfare state forward."
